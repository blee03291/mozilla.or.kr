Android

Firefox is compatible with Android 2.2 and above devices.

Installation requires about 16 MB internal storage and 512 MB of RAM

Give Feedback or Get Help

Although your device may support Firefox, we have not tested Firefox on every Android device. Tell us about your experience using Firefox on your device. Ask questions or give feedback on the Firefox Mobile Support Forum.

See a full list of supported and unsupported devices by visiting our System Requirements wiki.
